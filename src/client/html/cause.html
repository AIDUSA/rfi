The Cause : About AID

About Association For India's Development

Association for India's Development is a volunteer movement promoting sustainable, equitable and just development. AID supports grassroots organizations in India and initiates efforts in various interconnected spheres such as agriculture, energy, education, health , livelihoods, natural resources including land and water, women's empowerment and social justice.

Donations to AID are tax-deductible in the USA. It is a registered 501(c)(3) non-profit charitable organization with tax ID 04-3652609.

Why support this cause.

1.

Volunteer driven

Most of AID's work in the U.S. is done by volunteers in 36 chapters across the US.

2.

Accountable

Most of our projects are visited on a regular basis by volunteers. AID is a Charity Navigator reputed NGO.

3.

Low overhead

Over 95% of the funds raised go to support sustainable development projects in India.

4.

Leadership

AID was awarded 2011 Times of India Global Social Impact Award in Global Contribution in India. Several of our project partners are seen as role models in solving social issues and have been highlighted in shows such as Satyamev Jayate.
