A little on Our Program

What is this program all about?

Run For India is a beginner-friendly marathon/half-marathon training and fundraising program organized by the volunteers from Association for India's Development (AID). We train runners for their full or half marathon races and runners raise donations for AID and both beginner and experienced runners are welcome. Our tag line is each mile for a smile.

How do I join?

You can see if the US city you live in has a chapter and check out the list to see if it is a participating chapter in this fundraiser or you can contact us if you want to just run on our own with a custom page to raise money on your own..

What half/full marathons do you target?

This really depends on the city you are in and the marathons near it.
